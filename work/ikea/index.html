Work About Contact

Ikea

Brief

Create a new branding campaign for Valentine’s Day.

Concept

« Pick your Valentine » This concept is in keeping with IKEA values and its tone of communication quirky and funny.

So we rebuild a single woman flat as presentation rooms in IKEA stores. She’s a client at IKEA so she has a lot of brand products. And she looking for the perfect Valentine for February 12th.

So all along February month she has a lot of dates and she will choose the perfect one for the D-day.

We deflect IKEA tags replace price by the date of the rendezvous. Furnitures names are replaced by names of men temporarily until Valentine’s Day but also dimensions and features.

We play on the ambiguity. We don’t know if is Mark is the furniture or the man whom she met.
So we don’t know if she will choose a fourniture or a man for the 12th of February.

Target

Single woman not targeted on age but on state of mind. She’s free, modern and bouncy. She’s already a client at IKEA. She wants something disruptive for Valentine’s Day.

Media

Press : decoration and women’s magazines
