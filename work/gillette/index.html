GILLETTE

Brief

Set a new brand positioning in accordance with the new codes of masculinity Convert this new positioning in a Manifesto film Campaign for December 2018

Observations

To live on this mature market, brands are racing for innovation, always looking for improvements. In the face of all these offers, consumers are mindful to make the right choice: they tend to focus on reliable brands that they trust.
Meanwhile, the representation of men in advertising remains static and doesn’t evolve as quickly as mentalities. Male beauty brands always target the « manly status ».

Gillette is no exception. Since the 80s, his communication has focused on the idea of « man’s perfection ». It is based on three main axes: an innovative technology, an ideal stereotype of man, and success stories of brand ambassadors.

GILLETTE ENABLES MEN TO ACHIEVE EXCELLENCE

In 2018, at a time when the concept of “masculinity” takes on a “s”, it is important to consider men in their uniqueness. The man is in search of identity, and is no longer found in the representations made of him by the brands and the media.

Gillette, in contradiction with the new social codes, must therefore evolve with time.

Campaign goals

1
Translating « men perfection » to current codes
2
Adopte a new brand positioning
3
Stregthen equity brand

THE IDEAL MAN HAS EVOLVED

Gillette is no longer addressing only one type of man

"A MAN IS A MAN, HE IS NOT CALLED A MAN FOR HIS BEARD, HIS MUSCLES OR HIS VALUES. WE CAN NO LONGER CATEGORIZE PEOPLE LIKE THAT TODAY!"

Luc, 26 ans, Engineer.

He does not feel concerned by the old codes of 'masculinity': authority, manhood, success, power
He shares opinions, lifestyle on social networks
He’s searching for authenticity and reliability in brands rather than dreams
He needs to be accompanied and advised during the purchase process

Campaign concept

Gillette explains to its consumers that each of them embodies their own « masculine perfection » and helps them to highlight it everyday

GILLETTE RELAYED THE FOLLOWING MESSAGE

“Don’t let anyone tell you what perfection is, or what you need to do to achieve it.
By being yourself, by developing your peculiarities, you will be only more unique, and therefore more exceptional.
This also involves shaving, as hair can radically alter the appearance.
Gillette will help you achieve your own perfection»

GILLETTE HELPS EVERY MAN TO FULFILL HIMSELF

Take Biases

To shown the new vision of « man perfection » it’s necessary to take sides.

AVOID STEREOTYPICAL REPRESENTATIONS TO INCLUDE AS MANY MEN AS POSSIBLE

Giving a realistic representation of man creates a climate of trust between consumers and the brand.

DO NOT USE BRAND MUSES

The pursuit of an impossible ideal no longer convinces today’s men in search of identity. The target tends to appreciate a celebrity more than identify with it. Also, the use of a lambda person makes the speech more reliable than that of a muse

CHANGE BRAND COMMUNICATION TONE

The incredible epic of the alpha-male who lives more intensely than the others no longer likes.

DELIVERING A RELEVANT ADDRESS

MESSAGE:Gillette has clearly understood the issues facing men today, and gives them the means to assert their personality

PROMISE: Optimal shaving quality to cultivate your own

RATIONAL BENEFITS: Perfection: the consumer has confidence in the quality of the product. The shaving experience is comfortable, with precision.

EMOTIONAL BENEFITS: He appreciates the authenticity of the brand and it generates confidence. He identifies with it: gain in self-confidence. The brand accompanies them: proximity

REASON TO BELIEVE: Innovative technology/ Demonstration of different shaves by unknown people and with different profiles

TON: Light, uncompleted, encouraging

Manifesto

Broadcasting this brand manifesto on television will allow to strengthen the brand equity Objective: To show the male uniqueness, the unique personality of each person Succession of different profiles of man: at first they are shaving in their own way and then we see them in their daily actions
