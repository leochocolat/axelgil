Brief

Launch of a communication campaign to promote the new product «Sacrément Bon» for Heinz France

Observations

Heinz has been in the minds of consumers since childhood. There remains a real emotional link between the consumer and the brand thanks to its ketchup
But as they grow, tastes become more sophisticated and way of consuming change. Some people don’t take the time to cook, they just make food. Even though they are looking for speed cooking, they remain concerned about the quality of their meals by looking for healthy products.

That is why, as Heinz evolved, its face to 2 problems:

- the fact that people associate it too much with ketchup, a sauce that often reminds junk food

- the fact that it has an image of an American Giant that prevents it from proving its product quality.

But its expertise in tomatoes makes the brand legitimate to offer sauces for pasta compare to its competitors.

« I WILL BE READY TO TEST THE HEINZ SAUCES BECAUSE I ALREADY LOVE THEIR KETCHUP »

Morgane, 21 ans

Campaign concept

There is still a strong connection between Heinz and its consumers that could be akin to a relationship between two people.
This relationship must be nurtured and preserved so that it is always stimulating.

In addition, the creation of sauces and the means for Heinz to prove that it doesn’t rests on its laurels with Ketchup and seeks to evolve with the expectations of its consumers

Target

To promote its product, Heinz can count in consumers who already trust it

20-35 years old
Upper socio professional category
Urban

This person who consumes Heinz since time immemorial
This person who believes that ketchup Heinz is the best
This person who already consumes sauces for pasta

Take Biases

GIVE HUMAN DIMENSION TO THE BRAND

Telling a story by personifying the brand Allows Heinz to speak with authenticity to consumers with a message that resonates Sharing a message of more human values allows the brand to build trust

ADDRESS AS THE FAMILIAR FORM WITH THE TARGET

Allows to communicate with simplicity while giving higher to the personification Enables strengthening of proximity with the consumer

USE A QUIRKY TONE OF COMMUNICATION

Create a speech at double sens to create an ambiguity This will arouse consumer curiosity and give a humorous tone to communication

Delivering a distinguishing address

MESSAGE: Heinz has clearly understood the changing needs of its consumers, and has therefore created sauces specially for them

PROMISE: Heinz pasta sauces will delight you as much as his ketchup did

RATIONAL BENEFITS: A good and high-quality product to enhance its pasta dishes with simplicity

EMOTIONAL BENEFITS: Consumers will once again have trust in Heinz. Its more human and authentic message strengthens the closeness between both

REASON TO BELIEVE: Heinz is an expert in tomato, it is legitimate when he offers condiments and sauces based on tomatoes

TON: quirky and emotional
